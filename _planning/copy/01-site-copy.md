## WHAT WE DO

**Header:** We capture what your experts know and build systems around it.

### Discovery

We start by spending time with your team. Not running workshops or sending surveys—actually sitting with the people who do the work, watching their process, asking probing questions that force them to articulate decisions they've been making on autopilot for years. We call it workflow mapping, but it's closer to forensic interviewing. We even use recording devices and screen capture tools to catch the micro-decisions that people can't consciously explain.

One client had a step in their workflow they called "Katie's gut." Four hours of conversation later, we'd mapped a decision-making process built on 20 years of supply chain pattern recognition that nobody had ever documented. That's the kind of knowledge we find.

### Knowledge Systems

We build RAG systems that index your accumulated history—project files, correspondence, documentation, call transcripts—making decades of institutional knowledge searchable through plain language questions. Your junior employee can ask "how did we handle this situation in 2019?" and get an answer with source documents in seconds, not hours.

### Quality Pre-Flight

For companies where a senior person reviews every output, we build AI-powered quality checks that catch the repetitive errors before work reaches the expert reviewer. Your best person stops wasting time on problems that don't require their judgment and focuses on the ones that do. One client had 11 review cycles per design. We're building a system to cut that in half.

### Knowledge Capture

Wearable recording devices and custom software that capture expert decision-making as it happens—not in an interview room, but in the actual workflow. Your retiring specialist's 30 years of accumulated wisdom becomes a searchable, trainable dataset instead of a farewell lunch and a box of files.
